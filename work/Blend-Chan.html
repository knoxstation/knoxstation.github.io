Blend-Chan

Overview

For Blend-Chan, I wanted her to feel like a tiny collectible mascot. Cute, polished, and full of personality. The challenge was taking a very simple, stylized design and giving it enough dimension and charm so she felt like a real character rather than a flat toy.

I began with a clean low-poly base and focused on tight edge flow around the expressive areas. Light sculpting and subdivision softened the shapes while keeping everything crisp and readable.
